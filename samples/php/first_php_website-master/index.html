Modern Life Is Built on Chemistry

EIT Coperation has worked with the Chemical Industries Association and ITN Productions to help create 'Solutions for our Future', a series of short films that feature a review of innovation in manufacturing and industry. These news-style reports and editorial profiles highlight the very latest developments across the chemical sector.

EIT Coperation is a young company. It has grown to become a leading chemical company with sales today of around $60 billion. Most of our employees have spent all their working lives in the chemical or oil industry. We continue unashamedly to extract best practices from this very impressive group of people in all spheres of activity. We believe EIT Coperation is a refreshing place to work and we are prepared to embrace new approaches to business.
